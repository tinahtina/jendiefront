Help and Faq

1. What is Bimaplus?

Bimaplus is an Insuretech platform that offers seamless, automated and simple processes for all Motor Insurance solutions accessible to Insurance agents and clients in partnership with Insurance Companies in Kenya. This product is administered by Iplus Insurance Agency Limited.

2. Who are Iplus Insurance Agency Limited?

Iplus Insurance Agency Limited is registered company in Kenya by the Registrar of companies in 2020. It is based in Nairobi, Equity Plaza 3rd Floor Ngara with interest across the country. We are licensed and regulated by Insurance Regulatory Authority and a Member of the Association of Kenya Professional Insurance Agents(AKPIA)

3. What is Motor Insurance?

It is a term policy that expires after one year. It categorized into three; Third Party Only, Third Party & Theft and Comprehensive.

4. What is the difference between Third Party Only (TPO) and comprehensive motor Insurance?

Third party Only is a basic policy, which is a requirement through a parliament act that any mobile vehicle should have. Comprehensive Insurance therefore covers for Third Party liabilities and own vehicle damage in the event of an accident.

5. What is Digital Insurance certificate?

It was developed by Association of Kenya Insurers (AKI). This certificate is issued through digital channels like email and WhatsApp which can be the printed and be displayed on the vehicle windscreen. To verify is verifiable through downloading DMVIC mobile app or dialing USSD *352#

6. How can I buy digital Insurance certificate?

By getting a quote through our website or app you can follow the prompts and get your digital insurance cover in real time.

7. How long does it take to get the certificate?

Insurance certificates take within 2 hours turn-around time to be delivered on phone.

8. When can I cancel my cover?

You can cancel your insurance cover anytime.

9. What documents should I provide to get cover?

The basic know your clients details required are copies ID/Passport ,KRA PIN and logbook.

10. What is Insurance premium?

This the amount of money payable to an insurance company for issuance of cover.

11. How can I pay premium for my cover?

Premiums can be paid via MPESA, CHEQUE, BANK TRANSFER OR INSURANCE PREMIUM FINANCING.

12. Can I pay premium in installments?

YES, you can pay on installments for Insurance Premium Financing or installments offered by specific insurers.

13. If I pay on installments, does my annual expiry day change?

NO. your annual expiry date remains the same unless the cover is cancelled or issued on short term.

14. Why should I take my vehicle for Valuation?

It is important you take your vehicle for valuation if not brand-new to ensure that the vehicle is not under-insured or over-insured.

15. Should I pay for my vehicle valuation?

Most underwriters do not charge their clients for valuation fee, however in the event the insurer does not pay, the client pays for their valuation fee.

16. What If I do not take my vehicle for valuation?

The Insurer on expiry grace period for valuation usually one month, will downgrade the cover to Third Party Only.

17. Can any vehicle get a comprehensive Insurance cover?

NO, Insurers have low appetite for high risk vehicle such as old vehicles and some vehicle models. Acceptable age and vehicle models are dependent on respective underwriters, in most cases vehicle older than 15-12 years and below are acceptable.

18. What should I do in the event of an accident?

DO NOT ADMIT LIABLITY. Report to a police officer and obtain a police abstract, inform your Insurer through an agent if any should be within 72 hours, fill a claim form and submit with required documents.

19. Should I take my vehicle to any garage after an accident?

Refer to the approved panel of garage by your insurer, if not available inform your Insurer through an agent for advise on nearest garage assist or authorized repair for reimbursement.

20. Should I renew my policy after an accident?

NO. Your policy remains active after an accident.

21. Should I renew purchased optional benefit if utilized in the event of an accident?

YES. If you have utilized your optional benefit, this should be renewed on prorate basis.

22. How do I get my policy document?

Your policy document should provide to you by your agent within 14-30 days from inception.
